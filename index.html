Hello! I am a Ph.D. candidate in evolutionary genomics at the University of Toronto. My work primarily centres around understanding meiotic recombination.

I use both computational and wet lab approaches to better understand how recombination rate variation affects genome evolution using the model alga Chlamydomonas reinhardtii.

I'm also passionate about teaching programming skills for scientific research, as well as promoting reproducible and open science.
